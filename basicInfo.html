Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Giver
  • Author
  • Lois Lowry
  • Published
  • The novel was published in 1993 by Bantam Books
  • Genre
  • Dystopia, science fiction.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set sometime in the future in a utopian community on Earth.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told entirely from the protagonist Jonas' point of view.
  • Conflict
  • After Jonas becomes enlightened on lessons from the past through his new job he comes to realize the restrictions placed on those in his society's freedom of decision, individuality, and emotions. Jonas recognizes that no one should live in such a way and rebels against it.
