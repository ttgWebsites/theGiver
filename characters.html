Characters

  • Jonas
  • The protagonist of the story
  • Jonas lives a normal life in a utopian community, he has a mother and father, and a sister Lily, he is very content with his life but that all changes when he turns 12
  • He receives the job to acquire all the suppressed memories of the community from a man entitled "The Giver"
  • The Giver
  • He is the most important person in the community as he holds all the memories from the past
  • He can also see color, and feel pain unlike anyone else in the community
  • Lily
  • Jonas's sister, she is younger than Jonas, and is a very kind and compassionate girl
  • Gabriel
  • A baby who is brought home by Jonas' father for extra care
  • Jonas is not quite up to par with the other babies in the community and is scheduled to be released, or killed
  • Jonas' parents
  • Jonas' father is a caregiver, which means he cares for the infants in the community before they are assigned a family
  • Jonas' mother is a Judge in the community
  • Asher
  • Jonas' best friend, he is very carefree and an enjoyable person. He receives the job as assistant director of recreation
